Jeffer Einstein's Archipelago is a mystery and survival adventure where you play as Steve Howard, a captive who has escaped from the lab of the dangerous scientist Jeffer Einstein and must now navigate a chain of perilous islands to find freedom.
As you explore the archipelago, you will search for hidden clues, keys, and resources while solving a variety of riddles that challenge your logic and observation skills.
Progression depends on the lock and key system, where certain areas remain inaccessible until you discover the correct keys.
Alongside this, you must manage a hunger system, as your character gradually becomes hungry over time, requiring you to find food scattered across the islands to continue your journey.
Success relies on careful exploration, critical thinking, and resource management, as each solved riddle and unlocked path brings you closer to escape before Jeffer Einstein can catch you again.
