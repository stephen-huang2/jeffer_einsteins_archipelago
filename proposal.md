# The name of the Project / Repository
Name: Jaafar Einstein's Archipelago

# General Concept
A map game with puzzles and riddles, you can solve them to adventure on in the world and play through diffrent levels to unlock new land for you to explore.

# Story Line 
Protagonist: Steve Howard
Antagonist: Jaafar Einstein

You are going to be playing as Steve Howard. You were captured to an archipelago of dangerous Islands by Jaafar Einstein for human experiments, and you managed to eascape from his lab, now you have to explore around the map, solve puzzles and go through challenges to escape to safety before Jaafar Einstein cathces you and brings you back to the lab.

# Theme
Jaafar Einstein's Archipelago is a mystery and survival-themed adventure RPG. The game is set on perilous islands that are full of challenges, riddles, puzzles, and mysteries. While fleeing from the treacherous scientist Jaafar Einstein, players go to many locations. The game creates an interesting and brain-teasing experience by fusing adventure, problem-solving, and exploration.

# Target user
People that enjoys puzzles and riddles and likes games that involves brain challenges.
